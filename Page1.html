Dopamine and addiction

Social media triggers dopamine release, making users crave like, Notifications, and tend to scroll endlessly

Attention span

Constant scrolling and fast content reduce attenton span and make focusing harder over time

Sleep Problems

Excessive screen time before bed can affect sleep quality and brain recovery.

Mental Health

Social media comparisons can increase stress, anxiety, and lower self-esteem in teens.

Memory and Focus

Multitasking between apps weakens concentration and short term memory.

Positive Effects

Social media can also improve communication, learning, and creativity when used properly

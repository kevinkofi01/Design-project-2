Tiktok - Dopamine and addiction

Tiktok's endless scrolling system gives users constant dopamine stimulation, making it highly addictive and difficult to stop using

Youtube - Attention span

Short videos and fast entertainment can reduce attention span and make focusing on longer tasks harder.

Snapchat - Sleep Problems

Streaks and late-night messaging encourage users to stay awake longer, affecting sleep quality and brain recovery.

Instagram - Mental Health

Comparing appearances and lifestyles online can increase stress, anxiety, and lower self-esteem, especially among teenagers

Twitter/X - Memory and Focus

Constant updates and information overload can weaken concentration and short-term memory.

Discord - Positive Effects

Social media platforms like Discord can also improve communication, learning, and creativity when used responsibly.

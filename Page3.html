Effects of Social Media on Mental Health

These Statistics show how excessive social media usage can affect mental health, sleep, confidence, and attention span.

Psychological effect Statistics Description
Anxiety 41% Users report increased anxiety after excessive social media use
Sleep Loss 58% Teenagers lose sleep due to late-night scrolling
Low self esteem 37% Comparing lifestyles online can negatively affect confidence
Attention span reduction 45% Frequent short-form content lowers concentration levels
Chase of dopamine 70% Notifications and likes trigger reward pathways in the brain
Daily usage 2.5 Hours Average daily time spent on social media worldwide.
